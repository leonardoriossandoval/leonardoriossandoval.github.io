about me

My name is Leonardo Ríos and I am a Junior Software Developer with studies in Cross-Platform Application Development and a technical background in engineering.

I have experience in business software environments, working with SAGE ERP, SQL, Crystal Reports and Verifactu-related tasks. I started at Nortua as an intern and later continued as a Junior ERP Technician, supporting real company processes and custom software needs.

I have been also part of the Electronics Control Units in the Hardware Department at e-Tech Racing, a Formula Student team, where I collaborate on electronics-related tasks, PCB assembly, testing and validation of systems for an electric vehicle.

Currently, I am studying Computer Engineering at UOC and focusing on software development, databases, web technologies and automation. My goal is to keep growing as a developer and build useful, practical and maintainable software.
